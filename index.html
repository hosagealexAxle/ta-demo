▼ SaaS (Salesforce)
① Auto-pull: WO arrives in queue
▼ Schedule Assistant (SA)

③ Auto-push schedule + WO to TA
▼ Technician Assistant (TA)
④ Auto-write back to SaaS — WO status: in queue → scheduled
▼ SaaS (Salesforce) ✓ Loop complete
